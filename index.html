Welcome — thanks for testing our prototype

You're helping us trial RapidClaim, an idea for a property insurance service that lets people log a claim and get an instant assessment and payout offer.

Before you start

  • This is a prototype — nothing here is a real claim and no real money is involved.
  • The assessment result is simulated for demonstration.
  • Your answers stay on this device (in your browser) and can be downloaded at the end.
  • It only takes a few minutes. Please imagine you've had some property damage and want to make a claim.

Your claim details

Tell us a little about what happened. We'll assess it right away.

Why we ask

The cause of damage is the biggest factor in whether a claim is covered. Telling us upfront lets us check it against your policy straight away — so you know early whether you're covered.

Date required · approximate time if you know it.

Why we ask

The date lets us cross-check against weather records and declared events in your area. If your damage lines up with a known event, it's effectively pre-validated — no waiting to prove it happened.

What was damaged, and where in the property?

Why we ask

Damage type, location and extent map to our repair-cost benchmarks, so we can give you an indicative settlement range and confirm it clears your excess right away.

Show us the whole room first, then close-ups of the damage.

Why we ask

Photos are the evidence only you have. We compare them against thousands of assessed claims to validate the damage — replacing the slow first assessor visit.

Is the property currently safe and liveable?

Is anything getting worse or urgent? (tick any that apply)

Why we ask

This drives urgency. If something's getting worse we can authorise make-safe repairs immediately (e.g. use your own plumber now) instead of making you wait for permission.
